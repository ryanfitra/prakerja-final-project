About Us

What is Musi Coco Indonesia?

Taken from the name of the main river that flows through South Sumatera, where this river is the heart of the economy. Musi Coco Indonesia is a supplier of coconut derivative products that presents the best quality coconut in South Sumatera

Musi Coco Indonesia is at the heart of the country's coconut economy. We always provide the best service and guarantee superior quality in international trade. The coconut derivative products that we serve are Musi Coco, Musi Coco Fibre, and Musi Coco Charcoal. These two products have a high production scale and are competitive in international trade.
